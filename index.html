Manatees: A Majestic Sea Creature!

Manatee Fun Facts

Manatees, although called the cows of the sea are actually most closely related to elephants. This can easily be noticed when you look at the fingernails, both creatures have 3 to 4 fingernails. As manatees are marine mammals they breath through their nostrils.
